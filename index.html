Welcome to Your Guide to Explore Valais!

What would you like to do?

  • Ski

    Dive into the exhilarating slopes of Valais, where powdery snow and stunning Alpine vistas await. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, our top resorts offer an unforgettable experience.

  • Spa

    Unwind and rejuvenate in Valais’ luxurious spas. From natural thermal springs to modern wellness centers, immerse yourself in tranquility and pampering.

  • Hiking Routes

    Explore the breathtaking trails of Valais. From gentle walks through picturesque vineyards to challenging mountain ascents, there’s a path for every adventurer.

Ski Resorts

  • Verbier

    Renowned for its extensive slopes and vibrant après-ski scene, Verbier offers a range of runs for all levels. Explore Verbier and book your stay through our integrated booking system.

  • Crans-Montana

    Offering panoramic views and sun-soaked slopes, Crans-Montana is perfect for families and expert skiers alike. Discover Crans-Montana and secure your ski pass with our easy booking option.

  • Zermatt

    Home to the iconic Matterhorn, Zermatt boasts world-class skiing and charming village life. Visit Zermatt and plan your adventure with our direct booking feature.

Spa Options

  • Ovronnaz

    Nestled in the mountains, Ovronnaz offers a tranquil spa experience with natural thermal waters. Relax in Ovronnaz and reserve your spa day online.

  • Leukerbad

    Famous for its vast thermal baths, Leukerbad is the perfect destination for rejuvenation. Experience Leukerbad and book your thermal retreat through our site.

  • Saillon

    Known for its peaceful ambiance and therapeutic waters, Saillon offers a serene spa experience. Unwind in Saillon.

Hiking Routes

  • Aletsch Glacier Trail

    Embark on a breathtaking hike along the largest glacier in the Alps. Discover the Aletsch Glacier and book guided tours through our platform.

  • Val de Réchy

    Explore the serene beauty of Val de Réchy, a hidden gem with lush landscapes and diverse wildlife. Experience Val de Réchy and reserve your hiking adventure with us.

  • Bisse du Rô

    Wander along the historic Bisse du Rô, a scenic irrigation channel offering stunning views of the Valais region. Explore Bisse du Rô and arrange your hike through our booking system.
